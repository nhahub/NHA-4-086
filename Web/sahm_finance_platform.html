ABOUT THE PLATFORM

What is SAIA?

SAIA stands for Stock And Arrows Investing App. It's built around one simple idea: every stock is either pointed up or pointed down. SAIA's job is to make that direction obvious — backed by real data and AI analysis — before you put your money behind it.

📡

Live Market Data

Prices, volume and history pulled continuously from major exchanges — NASDAQ, TADAWUL, EGX, DFM and more.

✦

AI-Powered Predictions

A prediction model projects where each stock is likely headed over the next 30 days, with a confidence score attached.

↕

One Clear Up/Down Signal

No jargon, no clutter. Just a green arrow or a red arrow, so you know the direction at a glance.

💬

Ask the AI Agent

Talk to SAIA's AI agent directly — ask about any stock, compare two tickers, or get a plain-English read on the market.
